Telecom

Our service offerings in Telecom and Wireless Networks have been devised specifically to accommodate an extensive range of technologies including the latest 5G, 4G (LTE), VoLTE, GSM/EDGE/GPRS, WCDMA/HSPA+, CDMA/EVDO, and WiMAX .

Our seamless solutions are cost effective and alligned with our client's overall business objective. All the major wireless mobile Operators, infrastructure providers and Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEM’s) will lay testimony to the marvellous and yet economically viable business solutions developed by Velocitai.

When it comes to catering end to end solutions to the vast and dynamically demanding telecom industry, our service lines are further bifurcated to provide specific expertise to the necessary areas of work.

  • RF Planning

    • Velocitai is equipped to provide end to end solutions for RF Planning including RF Site survey, Pre- Construction Benchmark, iBwave Design, ROM BOM, CW Testing.

  • Network optimization

    • Our high trained teams of Network experts bring vast reserve of rich prior experience and relevant knowledge to comprehend and understand the bank requirements completely and create tailor made solutions that are produce desired output and are easily scalable to measure business value.

  • IP Operations

    • Creating working environments that are secure and protect the privacy of your data is our service specialty. Our commitment to security and protecting data privacy has driven us to create cyber security solutions and IP architecture that helps maintain a company’s operations system by planning, designing and maintaining IP networks. Our IP Operation services include both, support for new and existing IP networks. Our intrinsic knowledge of IP network architecture and its GPON Network framework allows us to provide seamless service network and architecture powered by latest configuration details.
