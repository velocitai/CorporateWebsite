Our Services

RF Planning and Network Optimization

One of the main reasons why telecom giants love us is because we provide all the service solutions operating under the same canopy of services. Our extensive range of services allows us to showcase our full scope of capabilities, skills, services, and performance tools required for designing and optimizing telecom networks.

Our operations team comprises of highly-trained and qualified RF engineers who bring to the fore decades of combined working experience among themselves sourced from different quarters across the world. Their expertise in multi-vendor and multi‐technology includes 5G, VoLTE, LTE, LTE-A, and CBRS.

Our continuous commitment to excellence through innovation and improvisation has motivated our research and analysis wing to develop native RF tool suite for expediting the collection, analysis, and optimization of RF environments.Our feature‐based parameter and neighbor list optimization has been compiled to align with any operator’s gold parameter list. Velocitai has developed a wide range of service proficiency including application of layer management strategies to support multiple carriers and technologies.

  • Our Alarm analysis is prompt at identifying and eliminating degraded elements within the system.
  • Our Call Trace Analysis for KPI improvement benefits accessibility, retainibility & mobility for the telecom carrier.
  • Thorough investigation and analysis for continuous improvement of KPI and cluster level optimization.
  • Extensive experience with Site Go Live procedures and best practices for all major telecom carriers
