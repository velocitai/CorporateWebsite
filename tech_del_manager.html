Associate Director-Account Management
at Velocitai (view all jobs)

Desired Skills and Experience:
  • Must have 5-6 years of work experience in a client servicing, sales & marketing.
  • Experience with international clients is an added advantage.
  • Excellent analytical skills, strategic thinking, and decision making ability in a fast-paced environment.
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills.
  • Effective time management and collaborative skills.
Role and Responsibilities:
  • Overall ownership for servicing assigned clients, this role is an amalgamation of account management and sales.
  • Account Management
  • Build and maintain progressive, strategic deep and long-standing relationship with the clients.
  • Ongoing client servicing, understand client needs and create service strategy.
  • Create & manage SOWs, estimates and budgetary approvals from client.
  • Lead hiring effort as per client needs and follow through to onboarding of staff.
  • Develop a complete understanding of key account needs & build a strategic plan to improve client profitability & overall results.
  • Ensuring quality and timeliness of work delivered to clients.
  • Timely reporting of client portfolio internally and with client teams as per account plans.
  • Close interactions with design, planning, project management and delivery teams for day-to-day work management.
  • Documentation and maintenance of Client archive and interaction history.
  • Collaborate with internal stakeholders - Admin, Training, Quality, IT, HR, etc. to deliver efficient and timely work deliverables to clients.
  • Improving the operational performance across the organization by analyzing results, monitoring variances, identifying trends, recommending actions to the management.
  • Identify and pursues opportunities for developing new business.
  • Ensure achievement of sales budgets for the assigned accounts.
  • Collaborate with all the stakeholders to maximize profit by up-selling or cross selling.
  • Negotiating contracts with key clients and meeting established deadlines for the fulfillment of each client's long-term goals.
