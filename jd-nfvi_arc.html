NFVI Architect
at Velocitai (view all jobs)

Location: Gurgaon
Required Experience & Skills:
  • Experience: 9-12 years
  • Able to design DC networks
  • Able to handle escalations and customer communication
  • Ability to configure and troubleshoot Nexus devices
  • Excellent communication and documentation skills
  • Ability to work in high-pressure environments
  • Networking: SDN controllers ACI, Data center Network equipment for Leaf and Spine Routers, TOR and BOR Switches from major vendors (Cisco, Juniper…).
  • Very good hands on TCP/IP, Routing, Switching, Network security, NAT/PAT, Firewall, Network Hardware/Software (IOS-XR, Nexus, IOS, FX OS).
  • Configuring & Troubleshooting Site-to-Site IPSEC VPN, Remote Access VPN, GRE
  • Switching: VLANS, STP, Rapid-PVST, Inter-VLAN routing, HSRP, VTP, Trunking, Etherchannel, Virtual Port Channel (VPC), Cisco Nexus Switching (5K).
  • Development and operations of infrastructure components for Network Function Virtualization in DT’s Data Centers.
  • Provide and manage the lifecycle of physical infrastructure and virtualized resources, hosted within the datacenters.
  • Hands on Red Hat Linux/CentOS, Ubuntu and Docker container.
  • Virtualized Infrastructure Manager (Cisco VIM Openstack) Deployment and Operation.
  • Accountable for the set-up, management and support of the datacenter tools and virtualization functionality.
  • Design and Lab Operations support (2nd line) for IT and Network infrastructure, storage and SDN controller.
  • Validation of new equipment (Hardware and Software) to meet the predefined customer standards.
  • IT hardware: x86 server architectures. Hypervisors (KVM), acceleration technologies (SR-IOV)
  • Storage: Solutions for Ceph storage including high-availability concepts.
  • CloudOS: Opensource and commercial solutions- OpenStack Technical Requirements:
  • Cisco Data Center products (Nexus 9000, Nexus 3000, Nexus 7000/5000) – Mandatory
  • Cisco ASR 9K (IOS XR)
  • Routing/Switching: BGP, OSPF, EIGRP, MST, PVST, Multicast (mandatory)
  • Additional: VXLAN, ACI
  • Certifications: CCIE Preferred
Perks/Benefits:
  • Attractive career path – no limits on growth for the right individual.
  • Great location
